How to Enroll

Welcome to Oluyoro Oke School & Modrasah! Follow these steps to enroll:

  1. Visit our campus at Oluyoro Oke, Ibadan, Ibadan, Nigeria.
  2. Fill out the admission form available at our administration office or online here.
  3. Submit the completed form along with the required documents (birth certificate, previous school records, etc.).
  4. Attend an interview and placement test, if applicable.
  5. Pay the required tuition and fees.
  6. Receive your admission confirmation and welcome package.

For any questions, contact us at admissions@oluyorooke.com or call +234-123-456-7890.

From A-Z About Our School

Oluyoro Oke School provides a balanced education, integrating modern academic curriculum with Islamic teachings. We are committed to nurturing knowledgeable and morally upright students ready to face the challenges of the future.

  • Academics: Offering subjects like Mathematics, English, Science, and Social Studies.
  • Facilities: Equipped with modern classrooms, science labs, library, and sports facilities.
  • Extracurricular Activities: Including sports, arts, and various clubs to foster holistic development.
  • Community Engagement: Active participation in community service and outreach programs.

About Modrasah

Our Modrasah program focuses on Islamic education, offering courses in Quranic studies, Hadith, Fiqh, and Arabic language. We aim to provide students with a deep understanding of Islamic principles and values.

  • Quranic Studies: Memorization and understanding of the Quran.
  • Hadith: Study of the sayings and actions of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH).
  • Fiqh: Understanding Islamic jurisprudence and its application in daily life.
  • Arabic Language: Learning to read, write, and speak Arabic fluently.
