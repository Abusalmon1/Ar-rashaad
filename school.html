Courses We Offer

Our academy offers a nurturing environment that fosters both academic and spiritual growth.

Islamic Studies

Our Islamic Studies program provides comprehensive education on Quran, Hadith, Fiqh, and Islamic history. Students learn the principles of Islam, aiming to build a strong moral and ethical foundation.

Primary Education

We offer a well-rounded primary education that integrates academic excellence with Islamic values. Our curriculum includes core subjects alongside Quranic studies, ensuring holistic development.

Secondary Education

Our secondary education program prepares students for higher education and life beyond school with a balanced curriculum of academic subjects and advanced Islamic studies.

Student Life at Ar-Rashaad Academy

We encourage participation in various extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, and community service, to ensure the holistic development of our students.
