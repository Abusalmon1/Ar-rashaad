About Oluyoro Oke School & Modrasah

Location

Oluyoro Oke, Ibadan, Ibadan, Nigeria

Our school is situated in the heart of Oluyoro Oke, a vibrant community in Ibadan, providing a serene and conducive environment for learning.

About Our Institution

Oluyoro Oke School & Modrasah offers comprehensive education combining both modern academic curriculum and Islamic studies. We are dedicated to nurturing well-rounded individuals prepared for both academic and spiritual excellence.

About Our Staff

Our staff comprises experienced educators and dedicated professionals who are passionate about imparting knowledge and values to our students. Our teachers are certified and continuously trained to ensure they provide the highest quality education.

About Our Students

Our students are a diverse group of young minds eager to learn and grow. We foster an inclusive environment where every student is encouraged to reach their full potential, both academically and spiritually.

Our Facilities

We are equipped with modern classrooms, a library, science labs, and prayer halls. Our facilities are designed to support both academic and spiritual learning, providing our students with the resources they need to succeed.
